The Manifesto The world is changing faster than ever — but there is one thing AI can never replace.

Artificial Intelligence is writing code. It creates art. It generates music. It trades the markets. It replaces jobs. It floods social media with bots.

Every day, the line between human and machine becomes harder to see.

But there is one thing AI can never replace: the human spirit.

THE LAST HUMAN ($LAST) is more than a meme coin — it's a movement. It represents creativity over automation. Conviction over algorithms. Community over bots. Freedom over control.

This isn't a fight against technology. We embrace innovation. But we believe the future should never forget the people who built it.

In a digital world increasingly dominated by AI, The Last Human stands as a symbol of resilience, originality, and the unstoppable power of real communities.

Every holder becomes part of a story

Not just another trader. Not just another wallet. A Human.

As AI continues to reshape the world, we choose to preserve what makes us unique: imagination, humor, emotion, courage, dreams.

The Last Human isn't built on fear. It's built on hope — hope that real people can still create the next great movement, that communities will always be stronger than algorithms, that humanity will always have the final word.

This is not just another meme. This is a digital revolution powered by humans.
